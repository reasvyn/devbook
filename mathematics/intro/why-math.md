# Why Math Matters

## Description

Math is the language of computation. From algorithm analysis to machine learning, every branch of software engineering rests on mathematical foundations. This document explains why a developer should invest in math and how it directly impacts code.

## Prerequisites

None.

## Content / Material

### Math Is Everywhere in Code

- **Big-O notation** — discrete math for runtime analysis.
- **Search & ranking** — linear algebra powers search engines and recommendation systems.
- **Graphics & games** — vectors, matrices, and trigonometry drive every pixel on screen.
- **Cryptography** — number theory secures the web.
- **Machine learning** — statistics and calculus train every model.
- **Compilers** — set theory and logic underpin type systems and parsing.

### Key Areas for Developers

| Area | Why It Matters |
|------|----------------|
| **Boolean / Logic** | Conditionals, state machines, circuit design |
| **Set theory** | SQL, type theory, databases |
| **Linear algebra** | Graphics, ML, data science |
| **Calculus** | Optimization, gradient descent, physics engines |
| **Probability & statistics** | A/B testing, data analysis, ML |
| **Discrete math** | Algorithms, combinatorics, graph theory |

### Common Misconceptions

- *"I don't use math, so I don't need it."* — You use it implicitly. Understanding it makes you better at your craft.
- *"Math is just memorizing formulas."* — Real math is about logical reasoning and problem-solving, not rote memorization.
- *"You have to be a genius."* — Most developer math is accessible with consistent practice.

### A Mindset Shift

Think of math as a **tool** — not a barrier. You don't need to prove theorems. You need to recognize patterns, reason about correctness, and choose the right tool for the job.

## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Big-O notation | A mathematical notation describing the upper bound of an algorithm's growth rate |
| Linear algebra | The study of vectors, vector spaces, and linear transformations |
| Discrete math | The study of mathematical structures that are countable or distinct |
