## When Creating Content

1. Read the parent `index.md` and sibling files first to understand what exists.
2. Write narrative content following the 9-section mandatory format (400-800 lines).
3. In Prerequisites, link to the relevant `philosophy/` or `psychology/` knowledge files.
4. In Next Steps, link to the next stage in the journey.
5. Use the first-person / second-person narrative voice вЂ” the reader is on this journey.
6. Keep tone direct, raw, and honest. No fluff, no padding.
