# CEO / Founder

## Description

What a technical founder or CEO of a software company should know — building products people want, raising capital, assembling a team, and navigating the journey from zero to sustainable business.

## Prerequisites

- Strong technical background — enough to build and evaluate technology decisions
- Willingness to take extreme ownership and ambiguity
- [Entrepreneurship](../../business/entrepreneurship/index.md) — comprehensive guides for technical founders

## Learning Path

### Product & Market

- `🔴 CRITICAL` Finding product-market fit — customer discovery, iteration, pivots
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Defining the vision — mission, values, long-term strategy
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Prioritization — what to build now vs later vs never
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Competitive analysis — understanding your market and differentiation
- `🟠 HIGH` Pricing strategy — models, tiers, willingness to pay
- `🟠 HIGH` Go-to-market — launch strategy, channels, sales motions

### Fundraising & Finance

- `🔴 CRITICAL` Fundraising stages — pre-seed, seed, Series A, B, C+
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Pitch decks — narrative, market size, traction, ask
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Investor relationships — finding the right partners, term sheets
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Unit economics — LTV, CAC, gross margin, burn rate
- `🟠 HIGH` Financial modeling — projections, scenarios, runway planning
- `🟠 HIGH` Board management — building a board, running meetings
- `🟡 MEDIUM` Cap table management — dilution, employee equity, ESOP

### Team Building

- `🔴 CRITICAL` Founding team — co-founder selection, equity split, roles
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Hiring the first 10–50 employees — culture, standards
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Culture setting — values, norms, remote vs office
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Leadership hiring — CTO, VP Eng, CPO, CFO, CRO
- `🟠 HIGH` Firing — when and how to let people go
- `🟠 HIGH` Compensation strategy — cash vs equity, competitive positioning

### Technical Leadership

- `🔴 CRITICAL` Technology strategy — architecture decisions for early-stage
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Build vs buy — when to build in-house vs use off-the-shelf
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Technical debt — when it's okay, when it kills you
- `🟠 HIGH` Engineering velocity — shipping fast without chaos
- `🟠 HIGH` Security and compliance — foundational practices, SOC2, GDPR

### Sales & Customer

- `🔴 CRITICAL` Founder-led sales — selling before you have a sales team
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Customer discovery — talking to users, understanding pain
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Customer success — onboarding, retention, churn
- `🟠 HIGH` Sales process — pipeline, demos, closing, contracts
- `🟡 MEDIUM` Channel partnerships — resellers, integrations, referrals

### Personal Effectiveness

- `🔴 CRITICAL` Resilience — managing stress, rejection, and uncertainty
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Time management — what only you can do, delegate the rest
- `🔴 CRITICAL` Continuous learning — reading, mentorship, advisory network
- `🟠 HIGH` Physical and mental health — sleep, exercise, support system
- `🟡 MEDIUM` Public speaking — podcast, conference, media appearances
