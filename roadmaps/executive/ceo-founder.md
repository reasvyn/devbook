# CEO / Founder

## Description

What a technical founder or CEO of a software company should know вЂ” from zero to sustainable business. Covers the full lifecycle: ideation, founding, product-market fit, scaling, fundraising, leadership, and exit.

## Prerequisites

- Strong technical background вЂ” enough to build and evaluate technology decisions
- Willingness to take extreme ownership and operate in ambiguity
- [Entrepreneurship](../../business/entrepreneurship/index.md) вЂ” comprehensive guides for technical founders

## Learning Path

### 1. Product & Market

The CEO's primary job is to ensure the company builds something people want.

- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` [**Customer Discovery & Validation**](../../business/entrepreneurship/beginner/customer-discovery.md) вЂ” problem validation, iterative pivots, the Mom Test, Sean Ellis test
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` **Defining vision & mission** вЂ” long-term direction, core values, company purpose beyond profit
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` **Market sizing** вЂ” TAM, SAM, SOM, bottom-up vs top-down, addressing serviceable markets
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` [**Competitive Strategy**](../../business/entrepreneurship/beginner/competitive-strategy.md) вЂ” positioning, differentiation, Porter's Five Forces, competitive moats
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` [**Product Strategy**](../../business/entrepreneurship/beginner/product-strategy.md) вЂ” prioritization (RICE, ICE, opportunity scoring), saying no
- `рџџ  HIGH` [**Pricing Strategy**](../../business/entrepreneurship/beginner/pricing-strategy.md) вЂ” value-based, cost-plus, freemium, tiers, willingness to pay analysis
- `рџџ  HIGH` **User research** вЂ” qualitative interviews, surveys, usability testing, behavioral analytics
- `рџџ  HIGH` **Product analytics** вЂ” DAU/MAU, retention cohorts, activation rate, funnel analysis
- `рџџ  HIGH` **Product roadmapping** вЂ” outcome-driven roadmaps, themes vs features, stakeholder alignment
- `рџџЎ MEDIUM` **International expansion** вЂ” localization, market selection, regulatory differences
- `рџџЎ MEDIUM` **Platform strategy** вЂ” API products, marketplace dynamics, network effects
- `рџџў LOW` **Hardware / IoT product considerations** вЂ” BOM, supply chain, manufacturing

### 2. Fundraising & Investor Relations

Raising capital is a core CEO skill вЂ” from angel checks to public offerings.

- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` [**Fundraising**](../../business/entrepreneurship/beginner/fundraising.md) вЂ” pre-seed to Series C+, mechanics and expectations
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` **Pitch deck & narrative** вЂ” problem, solution, market, traction, team, ask вЂ” the investor story
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` **Investor targeting** вЂ” angels, micro-VCs, growth funds, strategic investors, rolling funds
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` [**Unit Economics**](../../business/entrepreneurship/beginner/unit-economics.md) вЂ” LTV, CAC, gross margin, payback period, burn multiple
- `рџ”ґ CRITICAL` **Financial modeling** вЂ” revenue projections, expense build-up, scenario planning, runway
- `рџџ  HIGH` **Valuation methodology** вЂ” comparable analysis, discounted cash flow, venture capital method
- `рџџ  HIGH` **Term sheet negotiation** вЂ” valuation, liquidation preference, board seats, anti-dilution, vesting
- `рџџ  HIGH` **Data room & due diligence** вЂ” financials, legal, IP, team, cap table, customer data
- `рџџ  HIGH` **Investor updates** вЂ” cadence, metrics, narrative, bad news communication
- `рџџ  HIGH` **Board management** вЂ” building a board, meeting cadence, consent agenda, board decks
- `рџџЎ MEDIUM` **Cap table management** вЂ” dilution modeling, option pools, 409A valuations, waterfall analysis
- `рџџЎ MEDIUM` **Alternative funding** вЂ” revenue-based financing, venture debt, grants, crowdfunding
- `рџџЎ MEDIUM` **SAFE vs priced rounds** вЂ” mechanics, caps, discounts, MFN clauses, conversion triggers
- `рџџў LOW` **SPAC / direct listing** вЂ” alternatives to traditional IPO process

- `рџџ  HIGH` [**Regulatory & Compliance**](../../business/entrepreneurship/beginner/regulatory-and-compliance.md) вЂ” industry-specific regulations (fintech, healthtech, edtech)
