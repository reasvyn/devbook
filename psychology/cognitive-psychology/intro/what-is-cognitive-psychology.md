# What Is Cognitive Psychology?

## Description

Cognitive psychology is the study of mental processes — how people perceive, think, remember, learn, solve problems, and make decisions. It emerged in the 1950s and 1960s as a reaction to behaviorism, focusing on internal mental states rather than just observable behavior. Key areas include attention, memory, language, reasoning, judgment, decision-making, and goal pursuit. For developers, understanding cognitive psychology helps in designing user interfaces, debugging thought processes, and structuring work for peak mental performance.
