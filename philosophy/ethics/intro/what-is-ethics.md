# What Is Ethics?

## Description

Ethics (or moral philosophy) is the branch of philosophy concerned with how we ought to live. It examines questions of right and wrong, virtue and vice, justice and injustice, and the nature of the good life. The three major frameworks are virtue ethics (character), deontology (duty), and consequentialism (outcomes).

For developers, ethics has become increasingly urgent as software shapes every aspect of human life — from algorithmic bias and data privacy to autonomous systems and the ethics of AI. Understanding ethical frameworks helps engineers reason about the impact of their code on individuals and society.
