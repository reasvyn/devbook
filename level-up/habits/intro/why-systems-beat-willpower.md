# Why Systems Beat Willpower

Willpower is a finite resource. It peaks in the morning and drains through every decision, distraction, and moment of resistance. By evening, your best intentions are no match for your depleted brain.

Systems are what you build when you know your future self will be tired, hungry, and looking for the easy way out. A system removes the need for decisions — it makes the right action the easiest action.

This module covers the experience of building those systems from rock bottom. Rebuilding routines when you have zero discipline, trusting the compounding curve when nothing seems to happen, and designing a life where progress happens automatically. The goal is not to become a disciplined person. It is to build a structure that holds even when discipline fails.
