# What Is Psychology?

## Description

Psychology is the scientific study of mind and behavior. It encompasses everything from neural activity to social interaction, from unconscious processes to deliberate decision-making. Unlike philosophy, which investigates these questions through reasoning and argument, psychology uses empirical methods — experiments, observations, neuroimaging, longitudinal studies — to test hypotheses about how and why humans think, feel, and act.

The major branches include cognitive psychology (mental processes), behavioral psychology (learning and habit formation), positive psychology (flourishing and resilience), social psychology (group behavior), developmental psychology (change across the lifespan), and clinical psychology (mental health and treatment). For developers, psychology offers practical insights into user behavior, team dynamics, personal productivity, and the human factors that determine whether software succeeds or fails.
