# Level Up

The journey from existential rock bottom to a life of meaning, resilience, and purpose. This subject narrates the stages of personal transformation — understanding the void, finding meaning, building resilience, installing systems, and pursuing purpose. The actual knowledge lives in [philosophy](../philosophy/index.md) and [psychology](../psychology/index.md); this subject describes what the journey feels like and how to navigate each stage.

- [Introduction](intro/index.md) — the lowest point and the level-up philosophy
- [Fundamentals](fundamentals/index.md) — the map, the mechanism, and the developer's landscape
- [Awakening](meaning/index.md) — recognizing the void and deciding to change
- [Rebuilding](resilience/index.md) — getting back up and growing through adversity
- [Systematizing](habits/index.md) — installing daily routines that compound
- [Thriving](purpose/index.md) — finding mission and leaving a legacy
