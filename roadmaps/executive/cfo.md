# Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

## Description

What a CFO in a software company should know вЂ” financial strategy, fundraising support, board reporting, SaaS metrics, cap table management, and partnering with the CEO on business strategy and operations.

## Prerequisites

- Strong financial background ( accounting, FP&A, investment banking, or equivalent)
- Understanding of SaaS business models, metrics, and technology company dynamics
